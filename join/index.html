Cohort

Over the course of eight weeks, our Spring 2021 Cohort will individually connect with 30+ mentors and engage in community building. Our program will conclude with a public Demo Day where our teams will pitch their startups! If you want to accelerate your venture and join a select group of students, submit your application by our early deadline on January 30th or regular deadline on February 13th. 

Be prepared to meet from 4:00-6:00 PM CST on Saturdays (beginning on February 20th, and ending on April 17th). 

Mentor

Our mentors range from venture-backed student founders to investors to career professionals. Our cohort teams greatly benefit from the advice provided by our mentors. Because of the depth of our mentorship pool, our mentor commitment is for only one Saturday between 4:00-5:20 PM CST. If you are interested in becoming a Rice Ventures mentor or learning more about the service engagement, please apply below or contact our team.

Leadership

We are currently recruiting a Director of Outreach to join our leadership team. The Director of Outreach will oversee Illuminated, our newsletter, and our long-term mentor network pipeline. If you are interested in this role or if you believe you can add to Rice Ventures in another capacity, please submit a leadership team application.
